ABOUT US

Who We Are

NanoChar is a team of innovative researchers dedicated to solving indoor air pollution through cutting-edge biotechnology and sustainable design.

Our Mission

To develop affordable, biodegradable air filtration solutions that improve health and quality of life for communities most affected by air pollution.

Our Vision

Create a world where clean air is a fundamental right, not a luxury, by leveraging innovative biotechnology and sustainable design principles.

Our Impact

Reduce respiratory health risks, lower environmental pollution, and empower communities with accessible, sustainable air filtration technology.

INNOVATION

Our Revolutionary Technology

Biochar-infused nanofiber filters that capture pollutants more effectively than traditional methods.

Biochar Particles

Biochar Nanofiber Composition

Our unique filter combines biochar's natural adsorption properties with advanced nanofiber technology to create an unparalleled air purification solution.

Our filters capture up to 99.8% of harmful particulate matter including PM2.5, PM10, and VOCs through a multi-stage filtration process.

PM2.5 Filtration 99.8%

Our filters are 100% biodegradable and made from renewable materials, making them environmentally friendly throughout their lifecycle.

  • 100% biodegradable materials
  • Made from agricultural waste products
  • Zero harmful chemicals in production

Our production process is scalable and cost-effective, making it feasible for deployment in various settings.
