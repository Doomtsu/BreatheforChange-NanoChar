THE CHALLENGE

Addressing Air Pollution and Environmental Waste

Air pollution is a leading contributor to non-communicable diseases (NCDs), including respiratory and cardiovascular illness. Conventional air filters, typically made from fiberglass and synthetic polymers, contribute to environmental waste by ending up in landfills or being incinerated, releasing harmful pollutants and greenhouse gases.

Our Mission

To develop affordable, biodegradable air filtration solutions that improve health and quality of life for communities most affected by air pollution.

Our Vision

Create a world where clean air is a fundamental right, not a luxury, by leveraging innovative biotechnology and sustainable design principles.

Our Impact

Reduce respiratory health risks, lower environmental pollution, and empower communities with accessible, sustainable air filtration technology.

INNOVATION

Our Technology

NanoChar combines nanocellulose fibers with biochar to create a breathable, multilayer filter that traps particulate matter while minimizing environmental impact.

Biochar and Nanocellulose Composition

Our filter is composed of nanocellulose fibers infused with biocharâ€”a carbon-rich material known for its adsorptive properties. Together, these materials form a breathable, multilayer filter capable of trapping particulate matter while minimizing environmental impact.

Testing conducted using onion epidermal cells as biological indicators showed that NanoChar reduces exposure to pollutants comparably to standard filters. Onion cells exposed to NanoChar-filtered air retained membrane integrity compared to those in the unfiltered control.

Cell Damage Reduction 99%

Unlike synthetic filters, NanoChar is designed to break down naturally after use, reducing both air and solid waste pollution. The development process required low-cost materials and accessible fabrication methods, making it especially viable for underserved or off-grid communities.

  • 100% biodegradable materials
  • Made from agricultural waste products
  • Reduces environmental waste
